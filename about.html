Club Overview

The Science and Technology Club was founded in [Year] with the aim of fostering a passion for science and technology among students. Our club is committed to providing a platform where members can engage in various scientific and technological endeavors, collaborate on innovative projects, and gain valuable experience.

Objectives:

  • To encourage students to explore and pursue careers in science and technology.
  • To provide hands-on experience with advanced technology and scientific tools.
  • To support and mentor students in their projects and research.
  • To create opportunities for networking with professionals and peers in the field.

Focus Areas:

  • e-Yantra: Engaging in robotics projects and competitions to enhance practical skills.
  • Electronics: Exploring various electronic devices and systems through experiments and projects.
  • Computer Science: Delving into programming, software development, and computational theory.
  • Environmental Science: Addressing environmental issues through technological solutions and research.
  • Other Areas: Exploring other emerging fields of science and technology based on members' interests.
